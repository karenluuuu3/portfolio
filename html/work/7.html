隧歲 | The Tunnel

2023.01 #隧歲 #中正台 #中正台夜市 #unreal #unrealengine4 #ue4 #pointcloud #lidar #vfx #animation 「年年歲歲花相似，歲歲年年人不同。」 －摘自 劉希夷〈白頭吟〉 隧，寬廣卻非無限的空間。 歲，聚焦在一日的時間流動。 歷史遺留在新竹的某幾個角落，舊城區依傍在新竹市區周邊，新竹第一信用合作社、影像博物館、新竹州廳、新竹美術館... 走過繁榮與衰落，看著人群來回又離去，舊城區依舊在原地、依舊在原地送走舊人、迎接新人，彷彿培育著、呼喚著這塊土地、這塊土地的人們。 本作品以中正台夜市為主場景，將其掃描成點雲模型，分別以白天與夜晚的氛圍創作動畫特效，例如霧氣、光線、顏色變化、粒子的動態與樣式、影像後期處理等，並加入實境採樣的聲響，透過白天與夜晚的氛圍差異，賦予場域穿越時間與空間的視覺觀賞與聽覺感受。 "Tunnels—vast yet not infinite spaces. Time—focusing on the flow of a single day." Traces of history linger in certain corners of Hsinchu. The old town stands on the periphery of the modern city, embracing landmarks such as the Hsinchu First Credit Cooperative, the Image Museum, the Hsinchu Prefectural Hall, and the Hsinchu Art Museum. Having witnessed both prosperity and decline, the old town remains—watching people come and go, bidding farewell to the past while welcoming the new. It seems to nurture and call upon this land and its people. This work centers around Zhongzheng Road Night Market, which has been scanned into a point cloud model. Atmospheric animations and effects are created separately for daytime and nighttime, incorporating elements such as mist, lighting variations, color shifts, particle dynamics, and post-processing techniques. Additionally, field-recorded sounds are integrated to emphasize the contrast between day and night, offering an audiovisual experience that traverses both time and space.
