Game Story Severe disease has stroken the world for a period of time. The governments of countries try hard to have citizens vaccinated. They also strongly appeal to their citizens to wear masks in order to protect themselves and others. You have gradually noticed the situation of spreading disease, and you also find out that some regions are severe and some are not. How to Play We depart our game design into 2 parts (basic task and advanced task). We will finish the basic part, and the advanced part will be taken as extra mechanism if we are able to finish the project on time. In our game, on the ground in the game scene, there will be squares colored in red or green. Red ones represent epidemic areas, and green ones are safe areas. To be cautious, squares will change color with the BGM. Players will use a keyboard (WASD or arrow keys) to control the characters to move. If the player is in green areas, he will need to collect items (masks and vaccines).
