存在觀察 | Existence Observation

2023.10 #門 #窗 #絮語 #能量 #波 #時間性 #情緒 #存在 #構成 #劇場開幕 #unreal #unrealengine5 #ue5 #mixedreality #mr #arduino #multimediainstallation #niagarasysyem #niagara #pointcloud

現實世界的物質常以粒子的形式構成，這些粒子如同光束、塵埃一般存於空間，可能緊密、可能疏離，感覺不存在於何方、卻又無所不在，這些粒子是數據、意識、想像和祈望等不可見事物，以緩慢且難以察覺的方式存在且變化著，共同建構人類的真實經驗。 本作品分為兩個裝置構成、四個階段的影像。 裝置一以「窗」（門）作為一種象徵性的視覺媒介，並以展覽場地「藝空間」為點雲模型基礎，利用虛幻引擎虛構此空間，若觀者駐足停留，才能透過「窗」，一窺到這些原先無法單憑肉眼察覺的存在，並與之共鳴。 裝置二為地面的「絮語」，來自這扇「窗」（門）透露出這個空間所存在的意念， 階段零 : 尚無人駐足停留時，為一個通道，光圈由近而遠，欲引人入勝 階段一 : 門(劇場開幕) theater, dawn, optical light, tunnel, spotlight, fog, mist, outdoor, abstract 階段二 : 能量(存在感) energy, existence, voice, sound, religion, soul, fluid, abstract, cosmos, galaxy, nerve, line, smoke 階段三 : 意識、情緒(構成) language, thought, consciousness, emotion, floating, mind, line, mist, flow, desaturate, mind, abstract The material of the real world is often composed of particles, which, like beams of light or dust, exist in space. These particles can be dense or distant, seeming to be nowhere, yet omnipresent. They represent data, consciousness, imagination, wishes, and other invisible phenomena, existing and changing in a slow and imperceptible manner, together constructing the human experience of reality. This work consists of two devices and four stages of imagery. The first device uses the "window" (door) as a symbolic visual medium and takes the exhibition space "Art Space" as the basis for the point cloud model. Using Unreal Engine, this space is recreated, and when the observer pauses and stays, they are able to glimpse the existence that was once imperceptible to the naked eye through the "window" and resonate with it. The second device is the "whisper" on the ground, which reveals the ideas existing in this space through the "window" (door). Stage Zero: When no one is present, it is a passage, with a light circle from near to far, drawing people in. Stage One: Door (Theater Opening) theater, dawn, optical light, tunnel, spotlight, fog, mist, outdoor, abstract Stage Two: Energy (Sense of Existence) energy, existence, voice, sound, religion, soul, fluid, abstract, cosmos, galaxy, nerve, line, smoke Stage Three: Consciousness, Emotion (Composition) language, thought, consciousness, emotion, floating, mind, line, mist, flow, desaturate, mind, abstract
