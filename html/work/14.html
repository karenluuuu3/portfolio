垃圾車等等我 | Garbage Truck, Wait for Me.

2022.12 AR混合影像辨識、AIOT快速建構遊戲模組-防疫宅在家健身大亂鬥遊戲建置專案為例 AR Mixed Image Recognition, AIoT Rapid Game Module Construction - A Case Study of the Epidemic Prevention Home Fitness Battle Royale Game Project: [Garbage Truck, Wait for Me] Home Fitness Game App.

Introduction 由田野科技(Field Technology Ltd.)專案開發小組帶領2位研習生運用公司已建置AR混合影像辨識、AIOT快速建構遊戲模組，透過與NOBOLLEL合作共同開發自製年度遊戲【防疫宅在家健身大亂鬥】雛型之契機，實習企劃AR混合影像辨識、AIOT等跨域科技相關功能，混和規劃應用於該遊戲之提案及POC，學習與累積最新跨域科技功能於遊戲領域經驗。目前已完成企劃2款居家健身遊戲App －【垃圾車等等我】與【打擊垃圾車】，專案開發小組已完成開發，並於2022年11月30日上架OB版。 The project development team (Field Technology Ltd.) led two interns in utilizing the company’s established AR mixed image recognition and AIoT rapid game module construction to co-develop the self-made annual game prototype, [Epidemic Prevention Home Fitness Battle Royale], in collaboration with NOBOLLEL. The internship project focused on AR mixed image recognition, AIoT, and other cross-domain technologies, integrating them into the game’s proposal and POC. The interns learned and accumulated experience with the latest cross-domain technologies in the gaming field. Currently, two home fitness game apps have been developed: [Garbage Truck, Wait for Me] and [Smash the Garbage Truck]. The project development team completed the development, and the OB version was launched on November 30, 2022.

Game Planning Conceptual Framework • Fitness and exercise have become a trend in recent years, advocated for all ages. During the pandemic, maintaining health is important, but many can't go outdoors or to the gym and are stuck at home. • People want to maintain an exercise habit but don't want to spend a lot of money on large fitness equipment, and they may not even have enough space at home to use such equipment. • So, if it's possible to stay at home and play a game that can be installed on nearly every smartphone, and the game runs smoothly, wouldn't it allow more people to use and enjoy a fitness game app anytime? • After conducting market research on related apps and fitness articles, and discussing with the development engineering team, we came up with the idea to use image recognition technology to detect body movements, combined with gamification, to create a game app that achieves the expected benefits: a home fitness game app that is easily accessible. • Initially, we thought about incorporating props (such as AIoT or related fitness accessories) into the app, but considering that playing a game would require preparing additional equipment and accessories, which adds both cost and complexity, we decided to remove that idea. Instead, we focused on image recognition and motion sensing as the core foundation for design and development. • The game needs to be fun and involve full-body movement, and be suitable for all ages with accessible knowledge and skills (movements). For local Taiwanese culture, a game concept that would be quickly understood and engaging is chasing the garbage truck!
