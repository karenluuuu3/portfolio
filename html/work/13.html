水族魚生 | AquaVerse

2022.08 #水族魚生 #AquaVerse #ARExperience #AugmentedReality #ImmersiveLearning #TechInEducation

Concept • Educational significance Given the observation that augmented reality can be applied to educational development, daily life assistance, and more, how to integrate technology into people's lives in a way that enhances experiences and benefits society has always been a subject of discussion and practice. • Technological Interaction The dissemination of knowledge primarily relies on accessible content materials. With advancements in technologies like 5G networks and the metaverse, while text and images remain the main methods of communication, dynamic content such as videos and animations tend to capture viewers' attention and facilitate learning more effectively.

Introduction By developing augmented reality software, viewers can use their smartphones without the need for additional equipment to explore an underwater world. Moreover, they can interact with the fish on the screen, gaining knowledge about different species in an engaging and immersive way.

Prototype • Click on the main page and start the game • Select a fish in the environment (AR), and the introduction panel for the fish ill appear on the mobile device
