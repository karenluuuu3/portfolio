深海低吟 | Deep In Sea

2021.07 VR x HAND TRACKING x ESCAPE ROOM 在一個意外的考古歷程中，你將進入一個不可思議的世界 那艘船裡，時間的齒輪將停止轉動，維多利亞時代的一切停滯而存在著 那裏充滿魔力，還有許多神話中才會出現的傳說 你能在時限內解開遺留下的謎題、梳理整個事件的始末 並解救出上被禁錮在魔力下的靈魂嗎? On an unexpected archaeological journey, you step into an astonishing world. Aboard that ship, the gears of time have ceased to turn—everything from the Victorian era remains frozen in place. The air is filled with magic, and legends that exist only in myths come to life. Can you unravel the long-lost mysteries, piece together the truth behind these events, and free the souls trapped under an ancient spell before time runs out?

Story Outline After diving into the depths of the sea, the player—an archaeologist—hears a hauntingly beautiful song before gradually losing consciousness. Upon waking, they find themselves inside an unfamiliar ship cabin. Solving four intricate puzzles triggers the appearance of a figure claiming to be the captain of this ancient vessel. The captain speaks of the mermaids' wicked nature and grants the player a powerful artifact to counter them. In an instant, the once-bright room turns dark and eerie. Just then, a statue in the corner comes to life, introducing itself as a prince of the Russian Empire. He pleads with the player to help find his beloved, who was also trapped by the mermaids. Following the clues, the player enters a mystical mirror, where they witness the prince and princess's cherished memories. As the truth unfolds, the princess bestows a key to escape the ship’s cabin. But as the player steps out from the mirror’s world, a crazed mermaid suddenly appears before them. Story Branches: Path A: Draw a magic circle to defeat the mermaid. Path B: Use a hidden incantation found in the puzzles to immobilize the mermaid, allowing the player to be pulled back to the ship.
