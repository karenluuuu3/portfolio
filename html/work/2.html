Time Lapse

2021.09 SOUND ART x AUDIO VISUAL

Title: Time Lapse Media Transcription & Modulation + Audio-Visual Experimentation (媒體轉錄調變 + 音像實驗)

Introduction 疫情蒸騰，疫苗之亂、流言蜚語四起，如此兩年匆匆而過， 人們從最初的焦慮煎熬，到現今趨於平靜、已習慣居家自主管理， 與前線的醫護警消一島同命地抵禦這波疫情。 As the pandemic surged, chaos over vaccines and waves of rumors spread. Two years passed in the blink of an eye. People moved from initial anxiety and distress to a state of calm, adapting to life under self-quarantine at home, standing in solidarity with frontline medical workers and emergency responders to combat the crisis together.

Techniques For the sound materials, I incorporated news reports and various foley sounds from Freesound. Using Ableton’s effects, I applied modulation techniques such as echo, layering, and repetition to enhance the emotional depth. For the synesthetic experience, I utilized TouchDesigner to generate audiovisual elements, providing viewers with a dynamic visual perception.

Sections 1. In the darkness, ethereal and elusive lines shift unpredictably, gradually transforming in shape—like a virus lying in wait, silently lurking among the crowd. 2. Separation, infiltration, and fusion—much like human society, viruses exhibit similar behaviors, even transcending species boundaries. This interplay evokes emotions that fluctuate between unease and acceptance. 3. A slow descent into struggle—a suffocating tug-of-war, not only against an invisible enemy but also against humanity’s own interests and moral values. 4. The confusion surrounding virus mutations and the shortage of vaccines fueled a whirlwind of public discourse. 5. Even as cracks appear in human society, we continue to coexist.
