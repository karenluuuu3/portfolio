墨染熹微 | Smudge of Dawn

2022.12 #墨染熹微 #枯山水 #水墨畫 #inkpainting #glsl #shader #raymarching #shadingandlighting #computergraphics #digitalart #animation 枯山水，以山石白砂為主體，仿照自然的山海大川，從而實現「無水而喻水」的效果。 本作品以GLSL為創作工具，並以傳統中國水墨畫為質地，繪製出墨色暈染山中微弱晨光的景色。 畫面中，白砂水紋的「地」，是以三角函數對於畫面的座標運算出「波」的形體，並加入時間的變化，使水紋蔓延、甚至延伸至「天」，以增添畫面「生」和水墨繪製的感受，而後對水紋表面加入擾動使之有砂石的顆粒感，並在場地中融入三顆白石作為山石的導流。 Karesansui, or dry landscape gardens, use rocks and white sand to emulate vast mountains and rivers, achieving the effect of "expressing water without water." This work is created using GLSL, incorporating the texture of traditional Chinese ink painting to depict a scene where soft morning light gently diffuses over the mountains. In the composition, the "ground"—represented by the white sand and water patterns—is generated through trigonometric functions applied to the screen coordinates, forming wave-like shapes. By introducing time-based variations, the ripples extend and flow, even merging into the "sky," enhancing the sense of movement and evoking the organic feel of ink painting. To further enrich the visual depth, disturbances are added to the ripple surfaces, imbuing them with a granular, sand-like texture. Additionally, three white stones are placed within the scene, guiding the flow and serving as mountain elements in the composition.
