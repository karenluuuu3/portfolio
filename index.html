About Me

Hi, I'm Karen Lu, a creative technologist and digital artist based in Taipei, Taiwan.

I explore the intersection of art and technology, creating interactive installations, generative art, and immersive digital experiences. My work focuses on human-computer interaction and the emotional connections we form with digital media.

I hold a degree in New Media Art and have exhibited my work internationally. Currently, I'm experimenting with WebGL, creative coding, and real-time interactive systems.
