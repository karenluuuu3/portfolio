About

Connecting the World Through Art and Interaction. - Karen (✿ﾟ▽ﾟ)ノ 大學時期主修資工，因大三時接觸到電腦圖學與虛擬實境開發，而對將程式與藝術結合的表現方式產生興趣。過去曾在新媒體藝術相關公司擔任現場支援及系統架設，累積了豐富的觀眾體驗設計與技術落地的實戰經驗。 在創作上，我注重創意與技術的平衡，也積極與不同領域的夥伴合作。主要使用 Unreal Engine 與 Unity 開發互動程式與著色器，並善用 Blueprint、VFX 及粒子系統（Particle System、Niagara System）等。此外，我也擅長接軟硬體，如˙Arduino、Kinect、LED、投影機等硬體整合進軟體系統設計。 📍工作經驗：技術美術、互動工程師 📍接案經驗：網站架設 Hello and thanks for viewing my work! ☺️ During my university studies, I majored in Computer Science. In my junior year, I was first exposed to computer graphics and virtual reality development, which sparked my interest in combining programming with artistic expression. I have previously worked at a new media art company, providing on-site support and system setup. There, I accumulated extensive hands-on experience in designing audience experiences and delivering technical solutions. In my creative practice, I emphasize a balance between creativity and technology and actively collaborate with partners from diverse fields. I primarily use Unreal Engine and Unity to develop interactive applications and shaders, making full use of Blueprint, VFX, and particle systems (Particle System, Niagara System). Additionally, I am skilled at integrating hardware—such as Arduino, Kinect, LEDs, and projectors—into software system designs. 📍Work Experience：Technical Art, Interactive Engineer 📍Freelance Experience：website construction
