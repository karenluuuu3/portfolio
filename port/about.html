About

I am a creative technologist and interactive media designer based in Taiwan. Working at the intersection of generative software architectures, code systems, and physical design installations, my work focuses on building interactive bridges between human perception and natural spaces.

Through technical workflows involving TouchDesigner, Unreal Engine, and custom hardware sensors, I reshape digital graphics into real-time environments that respond dynamically to human behavior, curiosity, and presence.
